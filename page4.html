The Core Formula

C(n) — the block-coprime density — is the fraction of pairs (r, M) with 2 ≤ M ≤ G and 1 ≤ r < M where gcd(r, M+j) = 1 for every j ∈ {0,…,n}. A pair is block-coprime if r is coprime to every integer in the block [M, M+1, …, M+n] of length n+1.

C(n) = ζ(2) · ∏p prime (1 − min(n+1, p) / p²)

At n=0 this reduces to C(0) = 1 exactly (every pair is coprime to itself). As n → ∞ the density C(n) → 0 because longer blocks are harder to clear simultaneously. D(n) = C(n)/ζ(2) normalises by the base Euler product.

Stratification Identity

Every pair (r, M) belongs to a unique stratum d = min(r, M−r), the modular distance from r to the nearest boundary of [0, M]. d ranges from 1 (adjacent to boundary) to ⌊M/2⌋ (midpoint).

C(n) = Σd≥1 w(d) · C(n, d)

w(d) = #{pairs in stratum d} / #{all pairs} is the weight of stratum d.
C(n, d) = #{block-coprime pairs in stratum d} / #{pairs in stratum d} is the per-stratum density.
At d=1 every pair (1, M) has gcd(1,M)=1 so C(n,1) is near-maximal. As d grows, density falls — governed by φ(d)/d from the Gap Diagonal Identity.

View Modes
GridTriangular lattice — row M, column r. The natural domain of the stratification identity.
RingsConcentric rings by M. Each ring M has M−1 dots at angle 2πr/M. Supports per-ring twist.
Diagonal StripsRows are strata d. Width of each band = w(d), height = C(n,d). Direct visual of the decomposition.
Ford CirclesEach primitive fraction r/M (gcd=1) drawn as a tangent Ford circle at x=r/M, radius 1/(2M²). Encodes the Farey sequence geometry — tangent circles are Farey neighbours.
SunflowerGolden-angle phyllotaxis spiral. Pairs (r,M) enumerated in order, placed at angle k·137.5° and radius √(k/total). Density waves emerge as n changes.

Stratification Bar

Each vertical bar = one stratum d. Bar width = w(d) (relative cell count). Bar height = C(n,d) (block-coprime density in that stratum). The violet running-sum line traces Σ w(d)·C(n,d) from left to right — it reaches C(n) at the right edge. The gold dashed line marks D(n) = C(n)/ζ(2). Click a bar to isolate that stratum in the canvas. Hover to read exact values.
