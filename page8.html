Analytic Number Theory · Wessen Getachew

C(n) — Block-Coprime Density

$$C(n)\;=\;\zeta(2)\cdot\prod_{p\;\text{prime}}\!\left(1-\frac{\min(n+1,\,p)}{p^2}\right)$$
Natural density of pairs (r, M) with gcd(r, M+j)=1 for all j=0,…,n, rescaled by ζ(2) so C(0)=1. At n=1: C(1)=ζ(2)·DFT≈0.5307, where DFT=∏p(1−2/p²) is the Feller–Tornier Euler product (irrational — not a clean multiple of π²).
